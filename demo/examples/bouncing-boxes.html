Bouncing Boxes

Bouncing boxes can be faded out/in on page swap. This will mostly be non-positioned boxes below the defined swapBox. Consider the demo siblings at the bottom of the page and how they bounce if they are not faded.

Reload page without fading the bouncing boxes

Reload page with bouncingBoxes-option defined 

                
                    $.koolSwap({

                        bouncingBoxes : '#bouncingBox1, #bouncingBox2',

                    });

This is Kool Swap!
